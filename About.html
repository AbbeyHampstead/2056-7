Stay Safe

Here are a few tips about staying safe in this pandemic.

Call to action!

Wash Your Hands

Washing your hands with soap kills the virus that is on our hands. So wash them often or anytime you want to put your hands on your face because that's how the virus gets into your body.p>

Find Out More!

Wear a Mask

Since this virus is airborne, properly wearing a mask can help stop the virus from getting out into the air. But only medical-grade masks (which should be saved for doctors, nurses, and other essential workers) stop particles from the air getting to you.

Find Out More!

Stay 6 feet Apart

This virus travels through the air, and staying 6 feet apart is too far for a cough or a sneeze to travel. However, singing not only projects your voice but the virus so it can travel further than 6 feet.

Find Out More!

Stay Informed

Since scientists are constantly learning about this virus, new information is always coming out. And it's essential to stay up to date on that new information. It may save your life.
