Scientists are constantly learning about this virus and new information is always coming out. So, it's crucial to stay up to date on that new information. It may save your life.

CDC

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) is a national public health institute in the United States. Their main goal is to protect public health and safety through the control and prevention of disease, injury, and disability in the US and internationally. And it has been essential for informing people about COVID-19.

Center for Disease Control

WHO

The World Health Organization (WHO) is a specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for international public health. The WHO Constitution, which establishes the agency's governing structure and principles, states its main objective as "the attainment by all peoples of the highest possible level of health." And it has been very nessecary for informing people about COVID-19.

World Health Organization

Dr. Fauci

Doctor Fauci is an American physician and immunologist who has served as the director of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID) since 1984. And he has appeared on news channel after news channel to get the word out about COVID-19 and how to safe. He is the most crucial person to follow in this developing pandemic.
