Stay 6 feet Apart

This virus travels through the air, so it is essential to stay 6 feet apart from others whenever possible.

More Information

Why 6 feet?

This virus travels through the air, and staying 6 feet apart is too far for a cough or a sneeze to travel.

If the mask, why the 6 feet?

Both are nessecary because they both decrease the change of you or those around you getting COVID-19. So double the protection means double the chance you will not get sick.

Singing vs. Talking

Singing not only projects your voice but the virus so it can travel further than 6 feet. So its especially not safe to go to concerts or to sing without a mask around friends.
