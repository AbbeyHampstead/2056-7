Wash Your Hands

Washing your hands with soap can help kill the virus.

More Information

Why does Washing Your Hands Work?

At the molecular level, soap breaks things apart. So its breaking apart the COVID-19 particles.

How Often Should I do it?

Wash your hands very often or anytime you want to put your hands on your face because that's how the virus gets into your body.

Is there an Alternative?

If you want you can wear gloves instead. But make sure to not touch the gloves to your face.
