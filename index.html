COVID-19 Statistics

Wash Your Hands

Washing your hands with soap can help kill the virus.

Wear a Mask

This virus is airborne so, properly wearing a mask can help stop the virus from getting out into the air.

Stay 6 feet Apart

This virus travels through the air, so it is essential to stay 6 feet apart from others whenever possible.

Stay Informed

Scientists are constantly learning about this virus and new information is always coming out. So, it's crucial to stay up to date on that new information. It may save your life.
