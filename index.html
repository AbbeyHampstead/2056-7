Stay Safe

Here are a few tips about staying safe in this pandemic. More information and links are located on the top right corner.

COVID-19 Statistics

Wash Your Hands

Washing your hands with soap can help kill the virus.

Wear a Mask

Since this virus is airborne, properly wearing a mask can help stop the virus from getting out into the air.

Stay 6 feet Apart

This virus travels through the air so its essential to stay 6 feet apart from others whenever possible.

Stay Informed

Since scientists are constantly learning about this virus, new information is always coming out. And it's essential to stay up to date on that new information. It may save your life.
